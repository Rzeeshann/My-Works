About

My dream bike is the Honda CBR1000 RR. It is positioned against the YZF-R1 and also the Suzuki Hayabusa. The super bike is called Fire blade and comes with exquisite styling. It has been designed to give tough competition in the superbike segment. It not only has stunning looks but it also comes in many color options ranging from pearl white, black metallic to pearl siren blue. For the bolder types orange and red are also available. The Honda CBR1000 RR is poised to grab eyeballs in the world of street machines. In performance, Honda CBR1000RR proves that it is a super bike in every sense of the word. It is one of the most innovative super bikes ever built. The Fire blade is powered with a 999cc liquid-cooled inline four-cylindered engine with a compression ratio of 12:3:1.
